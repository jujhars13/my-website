# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 22 February 2026

This is the personal website of Jujhar Singh, available at [jujhar.com](https://jujhar.com). This policy explains what data is collected when you visit this site.

## What data is collected

### Analytics

This site uses **Google Analytics** to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects information such as:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website or search terms
- Browser type, operating system and screen resolution
- Approximate geographic location (city/country level)

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It is used solely to understand traffic patterns and improve the site.

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Hosting

This site is hosted on **GitHub Pages**. GitHub may collect technical information such as your IP address in server logs. See [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement) for details.

## What data is NOT collected

This site does not:

- Require you to create an account or log in
- Collect any personal information directly (name, email, etc.)
- Use advertising or remarketing services
- Sell or share data with third parties

## Cookies

The only cookies set by this site are those used by Google Analytics (prefixed with `_ga`). No other cookies are used.

## Your rights

If you are in the EU/UK, you have rights under GDPR/UK GDPR including the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. As this site only collects anonymous analytics data, there is generally no personal data to action. If you have concerns, please contact me via the links in the footer.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
